Name:

The name of the log is its identifier. If you're logging something that you've logged before, make sure you enter the same name as before to keep a running record of your activity.

Measurements:

Add any measurements (and their optional units) here. A measurement would be the duration of your run, for example. You can have many measurements; another example would be how far you ran.

Tags:

Tags should indicate something to do with the event you're logging. For example, if you're tracking weight lifting, a tag could be the exercise you performed while the measurement would be the amount lifted.
